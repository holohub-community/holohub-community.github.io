So, what exactly does a HoloHub volunteer do?

Volunteers at HoloHub aren't your typical kind of people.

Of course, each team member has their own unique talent(s)/skill specialization (which could include programming, management, moderation, video and photo editing, or simply being an active social media user, among other things) that they use to help the community improve and grow.

However, fully utilizing those specializations is dependent on the current project that the team is working on, and one member can effectively serve as the entire team that will handle all of the project's work (basically a one-man-army). Doing half-assed work is not an excuse given our small team size.

Before you apply to volunteer, please read the following below:

"We", "Us", "Our", "the Coordinators", and "HoloHub" are terms we use to refer to ourselves for the sake of readability.

To new potential volunteers at HoloHub:

Before your application to volunteer at HoloHub and its community is accepted, you must provide the Coordinators with some basic information for screening. This information is required for us to determine whether your volunteer application is valid and reasonable.

    The basic information needed:
  • Name
  • Age
  • Social contact
    • Messenger
    • Discord
  • Talent(s)/Skills of which you wish to contribute to volunteering

Please keep in mind that you must comply in a timely manner.

Any of our Coordinators will contact you for approval of your volunteer application, and you will be given further instructions regarding your application right away.

We respect everyone's right to privacy at HoloHub. If you are concerned about sharing your personal information with us, you can rest assured that only the Coordinators will have access to it. Any information about our volunteers will not be shared or sold.

Where to apply?

To apply, please visit our Discord Server and message any of our Coordinators. We are delighted to have you as a member of the HoloHub team!
